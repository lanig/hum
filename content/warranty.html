--- layout: default title: Hurom Slow Juicer - Warranty heading: Warranty ---

The Hurom Slow Juicer motor is covered by a 5 year warranty and the remaining parts are covered by a 12 month warranty.

With Hurom products, you are automatically pre-registered for warranty so there is no need for you to complete a warranty registration card. You will, however, need to retain your purchase receipt to prove the date of purchase.
