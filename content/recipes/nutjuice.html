Soy Juice

Soy milk is a great source of protein and a tasty alternative to cow's milk.

Ingredients

  • Boiled soybeans
  • Water or Milk

Method

  1. Mix the soybeans with the liquid, using a 1:1 ratio
  2. Pass the mixture through the Slow Juicer taking care to add equal parts of soy and liquid
  3. Run the soy milk through the Slow Juicer a second time to remove excess residue

TIP: Use the water the soybeans were boiled in to preserve any nutrients that might be lost by the boiling process.
