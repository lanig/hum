Carrot Juice

Carrots are full of beta-carotene, a type of Vitamin A which can assist with preserving eyesight and preventing cancer and ageing. It is also great for skin.

  1. Wash and cut carrots into long strips
  2. Put the prepared carrots into the Slow Juicer
  3. Rerun the juice through the Slow Juicer for less residue and a clearer juice

TIP: The orange colour of the carrot may dye the brush, but will wash out naturally when the next ingredient is used.

Wheatgrass Juice

Wheatgrass is abundant with vitamins and minerals and is effective in preventing ageing and cancer. It cleans the blood, increases circulation, lowers blood pressure and keeps blood vessels strong

  1. Cut the wheatgrass into three inch lengths
  2. Put the prepared wheatgrass into the Slow Juicer, one small bunch at a time
  3. Blend with orange or pear to reduce the intensity of the wheatgrass flavour

TIP: Wheatgrass can be easily grown at home or purchased from the internet and local growers.
